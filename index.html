Rochester in Action

Rochester Institute of Technology is my home. I'm in the Computational Mathematics program and I can't say enough about how great it is! I've met so many talented engineers, artists, and scientists there that I've lost count.

Blockchain Technology Club at RIT is a great resource for learning more about Bitcoin and the technology behind it. I am the sitting treasurer and am active in organizing our events. Come to a meeting and see what the hype is about!

University of Rochester is the sixth-largest employer in New York State, but it's so much more than that. The heart of intellectual life in Western New York is home to many a great minds. Not only that, but they act as a center for the community, bringing together people from all walks of life. I'm incredibly grateful for the U of R's relationship with RIT, together we can do anything!

Animal Service League is an amazing orgaization dedicated to helping those who can't help themselves (and who are cute and fluffy). It's a great place to volunteer and I encourage everyone to give what they can.
